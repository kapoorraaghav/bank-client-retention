Dataset Description

The churn modeling dataset provides a dataset on 10,000 bank customers. The dataset is normally used for the customer churn prediction problem. This prediction means to predict whether the bank customer will leave the bank or not. This information will be provided based on personal and banking information of the customers. The data consist of both the personal features such as age, gender and the geography of the customer, banking features such as credit score, account balance, and account tenure, number of products the customer uses and owned a credit card or not, active status of the account and estimated salary.

The dependent variable to be predicted, Exited, is 'whether the customer left the bank (1) or not (0)'.

It's a good dataset used for a classification problem, retention analysis of the customers and the development of machine learning models.
